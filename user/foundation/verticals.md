# Foundation — Vertical References
## Business-Type Patterns for Foundation Builds

---

## Law — CD/DUI

**Schema type:** LegalService
**Urgency driver:** DMV/Secretary of State hearing deadline — verify per state:
- Illinois: 90 days from arrest
- Colorado: 7 days from arrest
- Oklahoma: 15 days from arrest
Always verify before writing — deadlines vary and change.

**Emotional framing:** Fear → competence → accessibility. They\'re scared at 2am. The site
answers every question and makes calling feel safe.

**Trust signals:** Bar admissions, trial wins, specific case types handled, years in
practice, personal availability.

**CTA:** Always free consultation, always prominent phone number.

**Ad copy principle:** Fight/action messaging consistently outperforms credential messaging.
"Fight Your DUI" beats "Experienced DUI Lawyer." Test and document results per client.

**`knowsAbout` examples:** "DUI defense Illinois", "criminal defense Peoria",
"implied consent hearings", "field sobriety tests", "CDL DUI defense", "nurse DUI defense"

**Professional license angle (underserved):** Nurses, teachers, CDL holders, pilots —
DUI implications for professional licenses are a high-intent, low-competition cluster.

**Vertical filter for 8th Street /legal-marketing/ hub:**
Build only for queries containing:
- DUI, DUI attorney, DUI lawyer, DUI law firm
- criminal defense, criminal lawyer, criminal attorney, criminal law firm
Skip everything else — chatbot, healthcare, Wix, and other verticals are noise.

## Law — Other Practice Areas

**PI:** Urgency = statute of limitations. Fee = contingency always prominent.
Trust = settlement record.

**Family Law:** Urgency = children, assets, temporary orders. Sensitive — never take sides.

**Estate Planning:** Lower urgency. Framing = protecting family, peace of mind.

**Bankruptcy:** Urgency = creditor calls, garnishment. Framing = judgment-free fresh start.

**Immigration:** High stakes, high fear. Bilingual content often essential.

## Medical / Healthcare

**Schema type:** MedicalBusiness + Physician
**Trust signals:** Board certifications, years in practice, hospital affiliations, specific
conditions treated.
**Tone:** Calm, authoritative, reassuring — not clinical jargon.
**HIPAA note:** Never collect health information via Porch or contact forms without a
proper compliance layer.
**`knowsAbout`:** Specific conditions, treatments, procedures — granular beats generic.

---

## Professional Services
*(Financial advisors, accountants, engineers, consultants)*

**Schema type:** ProfessionalService
**Trust signals:** Credentials, years in practice, specific client types served,
results/outcomes.
**Tone:** Authoritative but approachable.
**CTA:** Free consultation, discovery call — not "book now".

---

## Trade and Home Services
*(HVAC, plumber, electrician, roofer, landscaper, auto repair)*

**Schema type:** LocalBusiness with service area
**Urgency drivers:** Emergency (burst pipe, no heat) or seasonal (AC before summer).
**Trust signals:** Licensed + insured, local family-owned, years in area, Google reviews.
**CTA:** Free estimate, always.
**Reviews:** Google, Yelp, Angi — not professional directories.
